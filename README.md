# 📖 Overview

Thousands of small businesses still rely on paper notebooks to manage customer credit (Udhaar). This manual process often results in delayed payments, lost records, and poor cash flow.

**CredPilot AI** digitizes the entire credit management process by combining **AI, workflow automation, and business analytics** into one intelligent platform.
